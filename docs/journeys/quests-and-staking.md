# How do I… earn and stake badges?

> **Use DIGHUb, collect badges, then put them to work.** The [Quests](https://hub.dig.net/quest) page has two tabs: **Collection**, where you earn commemorative NFT badges for things you do on DIGHUb, and **Stake**, where you lock badges you've earned into the DIG rewards vault to earn a share of **$DIG**. Your wallet is only asked to sign when you stake, unstake, or claim.

## Collect badges

Open **[Quests → Collection](https://hub.dig.net/quest)** with your wallet connected. Each badge marks something you've done — connecting a wallet, publishing your first capsule, registering a web address, and more. Eligible badges mint **free** to your wallet; the page shows what you've earned and what's still to unlock.

Badges are ordinary NFTs in your wallet — yours to keep, whether or not you ever stake them.

## Stake badges to earn $DIG

Switch to the **Stake** tab. It shows three things at a glance:

- **Reward pool** — the $DIG currently streaming out as rewards.
- **Your staked badges** — what you've locked in, and what each is earning.
- **Your claimable $DIG** — rewards you can take to your wallet right now.

To stake, pick from the badges available to stake and confirm the transaction in your wallet. Staking **locks** those badges in the on-chain rewards vault and starts earning you a share of the reward stream. The more of the period you stay staked, the more you earn.

## Claim your rewards

Rewards accrue continuously while your badges are staked. When you have $DIG ready, hit **Claim** and approve the transaction — the $DIG lands in your wallet. You can claim as your rewards build up; you don't have to unstake to claim.

## Unstake anytime

Your badges stay yours. Choose **Unstake** on any staked badge to return the NFT to your wallet, along with the rewards it has accrued. There's no lock-up period — unstake whenever you like.

## Good to know

- **Real on-chain actions.** Staking, claiming, and unstaking are transactions on Chia mainnet, signed in your wallet. Building and browsing the page is free; you only sign when you stake, claim, or unstake. A small network fee applies to each transaction, like any Chia spend.
- **Non-custodial.** DIGHUb never holds your keys or your badges — staking locks them in the on-chain vault, and only your wallet can unstake them.
- **You choose the language.** Like the rest of DIGHUb, the Quests page is available in every supported language; pick yours from the language selector.
